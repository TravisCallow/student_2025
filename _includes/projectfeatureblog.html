Program Function and Purpose (1.2)

  • My quiz feature allows users to study for their tests entirely online, making exam prep more convenient and efficient.
  • It works like digital flashcards — without the hassle of creating them or wasting paper. Simply open a quiz, fill out your answers, and receive instant feedback on what you got right or wrong.
  • Online quizzes streamline studying by providing immediate results and the option to quickly reset and retake the quiz as many times as needed.
